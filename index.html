Equate aims to promote the equality and professional advancement of women and non-binary people across Stripe. It will seek to support existing employees and help in wider recruitment, inclusion, retention, and diversity efforts. This ERG signals both to Stripe employees, as well as new Stripes, that we provide resources and career development opportunities for the growth and success of women and non-binary people at Stripe.
